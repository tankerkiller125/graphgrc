# SCF - PRI-04.1 - Authority To Collect, Process, Store & Share Personal Data (PD)
Mechanisms exist to determine and document the legal authority that permits the organization to collect, receive, process, store, transmit, update and/or share Personal Data (PD), either generally or in support of a specific business process.

## Control questions
Does the organization determine and document the legal authority that permits the organization to collect, receive, process, store, transmit, update and/or share Personal Data (PD), either generally or in support of a specific business process?
