    ISO 27002 - A.7 - Physical controls

    A.7.1

    Physical security perimeters

    Security perimeters shall be defined and used to protect areas that contain information and other associated assets.

    Mapped SCF controls

    • PES-01 - Physical & Environmental Protections
    • PES-02 - Physical Access Authorizations
    • PES-03 - Physical Access Control
    • PES-03.1 - Controlled Ingress & Egress Points
    • PES-04 - Physical Security of Offices, Rooms & Facilities

    A.7.2

    Physical entry

    Secure areas shall be protected by appropriate entry controls and access points.

    Mapped SCF controls

    • PES-03.1 - Controlled Ingress & Egress Points
    • PES-03.3 - Physical Access Logs
    • PES-04.1 - Working in Secure Areas
    • PES-06 - Visitor Control
    • PES-10 - Delivery & Removal

    A.7.3

    Securing offices, rooms and facilities

    Physical security for offices, rooms and facilities shall be designed and implemented.

    Mapped SCF controls

    • PES-04 - Physical Security of Offices, Rooms & Facilities
    • PES-04.1 - Working in Secure Areas
    • PES-12 - Equipment Siting & Protection

    A.7.4

    Physical security monitoring

    Premises shall be continuously monitored for unauthorized physical access.

    Mapped SCF controls

    • PES-03 - Physical Access Control
    • PES-05 - Monitoring Physical Access
    • PES-05.1 - Intrusion Alarms / Surveillance Equipment
    • PES-05.2 - Monitoring Physical Access To Information Systems

    A.7.5

    Protecting against physical and environmental threats

    Protection against physical and environmental threats, such as natural disasters and other intentional or unintentional physical threats to infrastructure shall be designed and implemented.

    Mapped SCF controls

    • PES-01 - Physical & Environmental Protections
    • PES-04 - Physical Security of Offices, Rooms & Facilities
    • PES-04.1 - Working in Secure Areas
    • PES-12 - Equipment Siting & Protection
    • RSK-01 - Risk Management Program
    • RSK-04 - Risk Assessment

    A.7.6

    Working in secure areas

    Security measures for working in secure areas shall be designed and implemented.

    Mapped SCF controls

    • PES-04.1 - Working in Secure Areas

    A.7.7

    Clear desk and clear screen

    Clear desk rules for papers and removable storage media and clear screen rules for information processing facilities shall be defined and appropriately enforced.

    Mapped SCF controls

    • END-01 - Endpoint Security
    • PES-04 - Physical Security of Offices, Rooms & Facilities

    A.7.8

    Equipment siting and protection

    Equipment shall be sited securely and protected.

    Mapped SCF controls

    • PES-12 - Equipment Siting & Protection

    A.7.9

    Security of assets off-premises

    Off-site assets shall be protected.

    Mapped SCF controls

    • AST-01 - Asset Governance
    • AST-05 - Security of Assets & Media
    • AST-06 - Unattended End-User Equipment
    • AST-08 - Tamper Detection
    • AST-15 - Tamper Protection
    • NET-14.5 - Work From Anywhere (WFA) - Telecommuting Security

    A.7.10

    Storage media

    Storage media shall be managed through their life cycle of acquisition, use, transportation and disposal in accordance with the organizationâ€™s classification scheme and handling requirements.

    Mapped SCF controls

    • AST-11 - Removal of Assets
    • AST-12 - Use of Personal Devices
    • DCH-01 - Data Protection
    • DCH-03 - Media Access
    • DCH-06 - Media Storage
    • DCH-07 - Media Transportation
    • DCH-07.2 - Encrypting Data In Storage Media
    • DCH-08 - Physical Media Disposal
    • DCH-10 - Media Use
    • DCH-10.1 - Limitations on Use
    • DCH-12 - Removable Media Security

    A.7.11

    Supporting utilities

    Information processing facilities shall be protected from power failures and other disruptions caused by failures in supporting utilities.

    Mapped SCF controls

    • PES-07 - Supporting Utilities
    • PES-07.1 - Automatic Voltage Controls
    • PES-07.2 - Emergency Shutoff
    • PES-07.3 - Emergency Power
    • PES-07.4 - Emergency Lighting

    A.7.12

    Cabling security

    Cables carrying power, data or supporting information services shall be protected from interception, interference or damage.

    Mapped SCF controls

    • PES-07 - Supporting Utilities
    • PES-12 - Equipment Siting & Protection
    • PES-12.1 - Transmission Medium Security

    A.7.13

    Equipment maintenance

    Equipment shall be maintained correctly to ensure availability, integrity and confidentiality of information.

    Mapped SCF controls

    • MNT-01 - Maintenance Operations
    • MNT-02 - Controlled Maintenance
    • MNT-03 - Timely Maintenance

    A.7.14

    Secure disposal or re-use of equipment

    Items of equipment containing storage media shall be verified to ensure that any sensitive data and licensed software has been removed or securely overwritten prior to disposal or re-use.

    Mapped SCF controls

    • AST-09 - Secure Disposal, Destruction or Re-Use of Equipment
